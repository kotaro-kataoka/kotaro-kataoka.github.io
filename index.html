The Lab Overview

Practical Networking and Blockchain Lab covers various aspects of Networking and Blockchain from the view points of theory and practice. We put more emphasis on implementing a base idea as a running system, benchmarking it, exploring the fundamental research problem, and developing its solution. We also interact with people inside and outside the institute to collaborate through the R&D, deployment, business of Networking and Blockchain technologies in the real society.

This lab was originally launched as “Practical Networking Lab” in 2015. In 2019, “Chainers Lab” was launched to work on Blockchain having Chaintope Inc. as a founding sponsor. In 2020, these two labs were integrated to Practical Networking and Blockchain Lab.
